Know Me Better

I am Swarnim Rimal born in 1997. I stand 5'6 tall.I am described as" Calm until I am not". I am passionate about art,dance,song,almost'anything that gives life a meaning. Growing up being the best version of myself has been my ambition and this has always been my motivation to work hard.
Throughout my life I was objectively beautified but my mother always believed in the knowledge I had inside me. To prove people wrong I had to step out of my comfort zone and challenge myself.This determination led me to join the field of IT. I was like an empty cup as I stepped into this world.Being part of this place and learning coding has acted like tea being poured in the cup. Coding gives me a sense of thrill but also a sense of control. This course has felt like a roller-coaster ride. Whenever I get it right,I feel like I am moving up and suddenly, one wrong move and you are thrown aback. Nevertheles,I am enjoying this ride and I can't wait to explore the next chapter.Let's find out what's in the store for me.
